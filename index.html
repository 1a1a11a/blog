I design systems that make large-scale data faster, cheaper, and more reliable.

I'm Juncheng Yang, an Assistant Professor in the Harvard John A. Paulson School of Engineering and Applied Sciences. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University, and my work spans cache management, reliable storage, microservices, and large-model inference — grounded in measurements of how real systems behave at scale.

NSDI 2024 · Best Paper

SIEVE: an efficient turn-key eviction algorithm for web caches

A single FIFO queue with a lazy promotion bit — simpler than LRU, with higher hit ratios and better scalability. Adopted across many open-source systems.

SOSP 2023

FIFO queues are all you need for cache eviction

S3-FIFO shows that three simple FIFO queues outperform eight state-of-the-art algorithms. Deployed in production at Google, VMware, and Redpanda.
